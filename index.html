About us

Bismi White Home Builders was founded with a dream built on hard work, honesty, and determination. The company was started by Nishad K, who began his journey in the construction field more than 28 years ago in Kochi.

Nishad started his career as a mason at a very young age. Coming from a poor background and living in a small house made with coconut leaves, life was never easy. Even as a child he worked hard to support himself and help his relatives. Through years of dedication, he learned every aspect of building construction from the ground up.

With experience, discipline, and passion for quality work, he became known for his exceptional skills and speed. Tasks that usually require two workers could often be completed by him alone in a single day. His honesty, craftsmanship, and commitment to doing the job right earned the trust of many clients and workers.

Despite many hardships and even facing a serious accident during his early years, Nishad never gave up. Those difficult experiences only strengthened his determination to succeed and build a better future.

Today, that determination has grown into Bismi White Home Builders. We specialize in building houses, villas, and commercial buildings with a strong focus on quality, durability, and customer trust.

For us, a home is more than just a building. It is a dream for every family a place of safety, peace, and happiness. That is why we are committed to constructing strong, beautiful homes where families can live comfortably and sleep peacefully without worry.
